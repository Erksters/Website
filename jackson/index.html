About Us

Who Am I?

Hi I'm Erick Saenz I am from Liberal, Kansas and I'm currently working on my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science at Kansas State University. What I love most about this field is it's puzzling nature. The feeling of creating or solving an algorithm is so satisfying to me. I sometimes find myself coding in the middle of the night or according to my friends "in my sleep". It's safe to say that I think I found my niche

One of my goals is to enable minorities in the United States to gain the confidence and experience available within Computer Science. As I grew up within my own community, I found that there was a huge lack of Hispanic role models in the field and no one supporting this successful career locally.

The goal of this project is to automate the extraction of chemical ingredients within published pdf's. Here we use the Natural Language ToolKit (NLTK) and PubChemPy for data preparation.
